Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our counseling and mentorship program

How much does the counseling program cost?

We offer flexible pricing plans starting from $49/month. The first consultation is completely free, and we have special rates for students. Contact us for detailed pricing information.

How are counselors and mentors selected?

All our counselors and mentors are certified professionals with extensive experience in educational psychology and student development. They undergo rigorous training and continuous evaluation.

Can I switch counselors if it's not a good fit?

Absolutely! We understand that finding the right match is crucial. You can request a new counselor or mentor at any time, and we'll help you find someone who better suits your needs.

Are sessions conducted online or in-person?

We offer both online and in-person sessions. Online sessions are conducted via secure video conferencing, while in-person sessions are available at our designated centers.

How long does it take to see results?

Most students begin to see positive changes within 2-4 weeks of starting the program. However, the timeline varies based on individual goals and commitment levels.
